Six ways into the operations center

TRACK 01

SOC Analyst

Triage alerts, escalate real incidents, and learn the rhythm of a Tier 1 / Tier 2 analyst rotation.

TRACK 02

Threat Hunting

Go beyond alerts — build hypotheses and hunt for adversaries already inside the network.

TRACK 03

Incident Response

Contain, eradicate, and recover from live-fire breach simulations against the clock.

About the program

What a Security Operations Center actually does

A SOC is the team that watches, decides, and acts — around the clock — when something in a network looks wrong. This program is built to put you in that seat early, with the tools and pace of a real floor.

  • Hands-on labs using the same tooling used in production SOCs
  • Mentorship from working analysts and incident responders
  • Batch-based cohorts with shared exercises and shift drills
  • A portfolio of incident write-ups you can show employers
